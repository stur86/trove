# Trove

**Trove** is a local AI platform for non-technical users, designed for resource-constrained institutions such as schools, care homes, and prisons.

It runs entirely on your own hardware — no internet connection needed during normal use, no data leaves your building.

## What Trove does

Trove lets an administrator define **Gems**: reusable AI tasks with a fixed purpose (summarise a letter, translate a paragraph, answer questions from a document library). Users pick a gem, fill in a short form, and get an AI-generated result — with no prompt-writing, no chat history, and no freeform interaction.

One server, many devices. Once set up, every device on your local network can open Trove in a browser.

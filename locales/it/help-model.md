## Choosing an AI model

Trove supports four Gemma 4 model variants. Only models that fit your computer's memory are shown in the list.

| Model | Speed | Memory needed | Audio input |
|---|---|---|---|
| E2B | Fastest | ~4 GB | Yes |
| E4B | Balanced | ~6 GB | Yes |
| 26B MoE | Efficient large | ~10 GB | No |
| 31B | Most capable | ~20 GB | No |

**Recommendations:**

- **E2B or E4B** — best for most schools and care homes. Fast responses, low memory use. E4B gives noticeably better answers for slightly more memory.
- **26B MoE** — good choice if you have 10 GB or more RAM and want higher quality answers without the cost of the full 31B model.
- **31B** — highest quality, but requires a powerful machine with at least 20 GB of free RAM.

After changing the model, click **Save settings** to rebuild. This takes a few minutes the first time.

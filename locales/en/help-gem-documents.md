## Document access

Each Gem can be given access to folders and documents from the document library. The AI uses this material when answering — you do not need to paste document content into the prompt.

Use the checkboxes to choose which folders or documents this Gem can see:

- **Checked folder** — all documents in the folder are accessible to this Gem.
- **Checked document** — that specific document is accessible.
- **Unchecked** — the document or folder is not visible to this Gem.

**Tips:**

- Only grant access to documents relevant to the Gem's task. Irrelevant documents waste context space and can produce less accurate answers.
- For large collections, organise documents into focused folders and grant access at the folder level.
- Documents must be uploaded in the **Documents** tab before they appear here.
